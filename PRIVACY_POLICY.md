# Privacy Policy for PulsePost

**Last Updated:** November 30, 2025

## Overview

PulsePost is a Chrome extension designed to automate X (formerly Twitter) posting. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

## Data Collection and Use

### What Data We Collect

PulsePost operates entirely locally on your device. The extension **does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data** with external servers or third parties.

### Specifically, PulsePost Does NOT:

- Collect personal information (names, emails, addresses, phone numbers, etc.)
- Collect financial information (credit card numbers, banking details, payment information)
- Collect health information
- Collect location data or device information
- Collect browsing history
- Collect user activity logs
- Collect authentication credentials (passwords, tokens, security questions)
- Track user behavior or interactions
- Create user profiles
- Send data to external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Use data for advertising or marketing purposes
- Use data for creditworthiness determination or lending purposes

## How PulsePost Works

PulsePost stores all data **locally on your device** using browser storage:

- **Posting queues** - Saved locally on your device
- **Settings and preferences** - Stored in your browser's local storage
- **Logs** - Maintained locally for troubleshooting
- **Images and CSV files** - Never transmitted anywhere; processed only locally

## X.com Integration

PulsePost interacts with X.com (formerly Twitter) only to:

- Open the X.com posting interface
- Automate the posting of content you provide
- Simulate user interaction through DOM manipulation

**Important:** Your X.com login credentials and session data remain in your browser and are never accessed or transmitted by PulsePost.

## Permissions Explained

PulsePost requests the following permissions:

- **tabs** - To open and manage tabs for posting
- **storage** - To save your settings and queue locally
- **scripting** - To interact with X.com's interface
- **activeTab** - To activate tabs during posting operations
- **alarms** - To schedule delays between posts
- **unlimitedStorage** - To store large image files and queues without quota limits
- **Host permissions (x.com, twitter.com)** - To interact with X.com for posting

All these permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality.
